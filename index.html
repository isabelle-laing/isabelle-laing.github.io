About

I'm an undergraduate researcher at the University of Toronto specialising in Astronomy & Physics with a Mathematics minor. My work sits at the intersection of machine learning and galactic archaeology — using neural networks to trace the Milky Way's assembly history through stellar chemistry. I'm also exploring dark matter's role in shaping stellar streams.
